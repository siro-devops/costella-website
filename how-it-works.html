How it works

Six steps from discovery to delivery. No guesswork. No hidden fees.

1

Discovery call (free, 30 min)

We hop on a call to understand your product, quantity, budget, and timeline. No pressure. No obligation. We'll recommend the right service tier for your needs.

What we need from you: Product idea, target price range, quantity, and any must-have specifications.
2

Engagement letter + retainer invoice

You sign a simple one-page agreement. We invoice the retainer (credited against final commission). No work starts until this is signed — protects both of us.

Time: 15 minutes to review and sign.
3

Spec briefing session (paid, 60 min)

A dedicated hour to go deep on your exact specifications — certifications, packaging, labelling, delivery terms, and anything else that matters. This is where we prevent costly mistakes.

You'll leave with: A complete spec sheet we'll use to brief suppliers.
4

Supplier shortlist & quotes delivered

We research, vet, and contact suppliers on your behalf. You receive a comparison sheet with 3–5+ vetted options — prices, lead times, payment terms, and our risk rating for each.

Timeline: 5–7 business days from spec briefing.
5

Order placement & follow-through

You pick your preferred supplier. We handle all communication, spec confirmations, sample approvals, and pre-shipment checks. You pay the supplier directly — we never hold your money.

Commission invoice: Sent when you confirm your order to Costella in writing. Retainer credited against this amount.
6

Post-order debrief

30 days after delivery, we check in. Did everything arrive as expected? Any issues? This flags problems early and builds a relationship for your next order.

Repeat clients: 0.5% commission discount from your 3rd order onward.

⏱️ Typical timeline

  • Discovery call: same day
  • Spec briefing: within 3 days of retainer
  • Quotes delivered: 5–7 business days
  • Full order cycle: 4–8 weeks (depends on product)

💰 What you pay & when

  • Retainer: 50% upfront, 50% before spec briefing
  • Commission: at order confirmation (retainer credited)
  • Sample costs: paid directly to supplier (+$50 handling if we coordinate)

🛡️ What we never do

  • Hold your supplier payment
  • Take commissions or kickbacks from suppliers
  • Share your specifications with competitors
