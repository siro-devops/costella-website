How it works

Six steps from discovery to delivery. No guesswork. No hidden fees.

1

Discovery call (free, 30 min)

We hop on a call to understand your product, quantity, budget, and timeline. No pressure. No obligation. We'll recommend the right service tier for your needs.

Cost: Free
2

Engagement letter + retainer invoice (50%)

You sign a simple one-page agreement. We invoice 50% of the retainer. No work starts until this is signed, protects both of us.

Payment: 50% of retainer due now. Example: Business tier = $175 due.
3

Spec briefing session + remaining retainer (50%)

A dedicated hour to go deep on your exact specifications, certifications, packaging, labelling, delivery terms, and anything else that matters. The remaining 50% of retainer is due before this session.

Payment: Remaining 50% of retainer due. Example: Business tier = $175 due. Total retainer paid: $350.
4

Supplier shortlist & quotes delivered

We research, vet, and contact suppliers on your behalf. You receive a comparison sheet with 3–5+ vetted options, prices, lead times, payment terms, and our risk rating for each.

Timeline: 5–7 business days from spec briefing. Cost covered by retainer.
5

Order placement & commission invoice

You pick your preferred supplier. We handle all communication, spec confirmations, sample approvals, and pre-shipment checks. You pay the supplier directly, we never hold your money. Commission invoice sent when you confirm your order in writing.

Payment: Commission due now (retainer already paid separately). Example: $10,000 order at 6% = $600 commission.
6

Post-order debrief

30 days after delivery, we check in. Did everything arrive as expected? Any issues? This flags problems early and builds a relationship for your next order.

Repeat clients: 0.5% commission discount from your 3rd order onward.

⏱️ Typical timeline

  • Discovery call: same day
  • Retainer paid: within 3 days
  • Spec briefing: after retainer paid
  • Quotes delivered: 5–7 business days
  • Full order cycle: 4–8 weeks

💰 Total fee breakdown

  • Retainer: paid upfront (50/50 split)
  • Commission: paid at order confirmation
  • Total = Retainer + Commission
  • No credits. No deductions.

🛡️ What we never do

  • Hold your supplier payment
  • Take commissions from suppliers
  • Share your specifications
