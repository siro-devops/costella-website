How our pricing works

Retainer

Covers upfront sourcing work: spec briefing session, supplier research, vetting, and quote collection. Paid 50% after signing, 50% before spec briefing. Non-refundable, work is performed regardless of order outcome.

Commission

Paid in addition to retainer when you confirm your order. Calculated as percentage of factory invoice value (FOB). Minimum commission $300 per order.

Payment summary: Retainer + Commission = total fee. No credits. No deductions.
Example (Business tier): $350 retainer + 6% commission on $10,000 order = $350 + $600 = $950 total
