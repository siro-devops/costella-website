Costella was built because too many Nigerian businesses were losing money to wrong specs, unreliable suppliers, and manufacturers who sent something entirely different from what was ordered.

Our Story

Built from a real problem

Nigerian businesses import billions of naira worth of equipment and machinery from China every year. Yet most of them have no reliable way to find the right manufacturer, confirm specifications, or protect themselves from substandard goods.

They rely on Alibaba listings and prayer. They send money to suppliers they've never spoken to. They receive goods that don't match what they ordered — and have no recourse.

Costella was created to solve this. We act as your procurement desk in China — speaking the language of factories so you don't have to, and protecting your order every step of the way.

"We speak to factories so you don't have to."

Mission

To eliminate the risk, cost, and confusion of sourcing equipment and machinery from China for African businesses.

Vision

To be the most trusted China procurement advisory firm for businesses across Africa.

What We Stand For

Our core values

01

Transparency

You see every quote, every supplier, and every decision. We never recommend a manufacturer we haven't verified ourselves.

02

Client Protection

We never hold your money. We never cut side deals with suppliers. Our only incentive is to get you the right product at the right price.

03

Precision

We challenge vague specifications, document everything in writing, and confirm every detail before production starts.

04

Accountability

Every engagement begins with a signed proposal. We are accountable to what we promised, and we follow through on every order.

05

Local Context

We understand the Nigerian business environment, import regulations, and what your end market actually needs — something no overseas agent can offer.

06

Long-term Partnership

We don't just complete orders. We build relationships. Our goal is to become your permanent procurement desk for every China order.
