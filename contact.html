Common questions

How long does sourcing take?
A supplier shortlist typically takes 48–72 hours. Full order management depends on the manufacturer's lead time, usually 2–6 weeks from order confirmation.
Do you handle shipping to Nigeria?
We coordinate with trusted freight forwarders who handle shipping to Nigerian ports. Freight costs are separate from our advisory fee.
What if the goods don't match the spec?
We conduct pre-shipment quality checks and confirm spec compliance before anything ships. If issues arise, we negotiate with the supplier on your behalf.
What's the minimum order value?
We typically work with orders of $5,000 and above. For smaller orders, a minimum commission of $300 applies regardless of order value.
